The **external price** is a separate value included alongside the oracle and mark prices in
relayer updates. It is the last externally derived SMM print.

- **When Chinese metals markets are open**, the external price equals the
  [oracle price](/market-mechanics/oracle-price).
- **When they are closed**, the external price stays fixed at the last SMM print while the
  oracle advances via its internal pricing mechanism.

The external price is the anchor that internal pricing references during closures. On reopen,
the external price updates to the live SMM print and the oracle reverts to it.
