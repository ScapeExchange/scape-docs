## Overview

Funding keeps the perp aligned with the oracle through periodic peer-to-peer transfers. When
the perp trades above the oracle, longs pay shorts; when it trades below, shorts pay longs.
Funding is paid **hourly** on the mark/index spread and settles to each position holder's
margin balance every hour.

## Technical Details

| Parameter | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Interval | Hourly |
| Cadence | Hyperliquid standard |
| Cap | ±4% / hour |
| Impact notional | 1,000 |

The funding rate follows the Hyperliquid formula:

$$
F = 0.5\,\big[\,P + \text{clamp}(r - P,\, -0.0005,\, 0.0005)\,\big]
$$

where:

- $P$ = average premium index = `impact_price_difference / oracle_price`
- $r$ = interest rate
- `impact_price_difference = max(impact_bid − oracle, 0) − max(oracle − impact_ask, 0)`

The funding payment is:

$$
\text{payment} = \text{position size} \times \text{oracle price} \times F
$$

## Off-Hours Funding

When SMM is dark, the oracle holds the last print while the perp continues to trade. Funding
does the work of closing the gap: as the perp mark drifts from the held anchor, the premium
widens and funding flows from the rich side to the cheap side. On reopen, any remaining
dislocation pays through funding rather than through liquidations.
