The oracle serves two functions:

1. The reference price for funding.
2. A direct input to the mark price.

Scape extends a metals oracle to operate 24/7, beyond the trading sessions of the Shanghai
underlying. When Chinese metals markets are open, the oracle follows the external SMM print.
When they are closed, an internal mechanism takes over.

## External Pricing

The oracle is derived from [Shanghai Metals Market](https://www.metal.com) (SMM) CNY composite
spot feeds, assessed on a 500ms cadence. [SEDA Protocol](https://seda.xyz) validates and
pushes the price to Hyperliquid every ~3 seconds. No FX conversion is applied — the oracle is
CNY-quoted end to end.

While SMM is publishing, the externally derived CNY price is transmitted as the oracle price.

## Internal Pricing

Chinese metals markets close overnight (UTC), on weekends, and on national holidays — gaps of
up to 10 days. During closure:

- The oracle **holds the last printed SMM value** as its anchor.
- A continuous-time EMA (CT-EMA) on the perp mid bounds drift from that held anchor.

The Hyperliquid order book drives discovery during the closure. On reopen, SMM resumes and any
dislocation between the perp and the fresh print pays through [funding](/market-mechanics/funding).
