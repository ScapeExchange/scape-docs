Scape lists three Chinese metals perpetuals. All are CNY-quoted, USDC-settled quanto contracts.

| Market | Underlying | Quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **SHAG** | Shanghai gold, Au 99.95% | CNY/gram |
| **SHAU** | Shanghai silver, Ag 99.99% | CNY/kg |
| **SHCU** | Shanghai copper, Cu-CATH-1 | CNY/tonne |

<Note>
  Notional per contract is calculated at reference oracle levels: SHAG 1,000 CNY/g, SHAU

</Note>

## External Coverage

External prices are derived from [Shanghai Metals Market](https://www.metal.com) (SMM) CNY
composite spot feeds on a 500ms cadence, validated and pushed to Hyperliquid via
[SEDA Protocol](https://seda.xyz) every ~3 seconds. No FX conversion is applied.

SMM publishes during Shanghai sessions. Coverage is dark during:

- Weekends (UTC).
- Published Chinese national holidays (up to 10 days).
- The 07:00–13:00 UTC mid-day gap.
- The 18:30–01:00 UTC overnight gap (between the SHFE day and night sessions).

During dark windows, the oracle holds the last SMM print and the perp continues to trade. See
[Oracle Price](/market-mechanics/oracle-price) for the internal pricing mechanism.

### Published Holidays

SMM is dark across mainland Chinese public holidays. The recognized 2026 holidays are below.
The longest closures — Spring Festival and National Day — can leave the oracle held for up to
10 days.

| Holiday | 2026 Dates |
| --- | --- |
| New Year's Day | Jan 1 |
| Spring Festival (Chinese New Year) | Feb 16 – Feb 22 |
| Qingming (Tomb-Sweeping Day) | Apr 4 – Apr 6 |
| Labour Day | May 1 – May 5 |
| Dragon Boat Festival | Jun 19 – Jun 21 |
| Mid-Autumn Festival | Sep 25 – Sep 27 |
| National Day (Golden Week) | Oct 1 – Oct 7 |

<Note>
  Exact closure windows — including any adjusted makeup working days — follow the official
  [SHFE](https://www.shfe.com.cn) holiday schedule and should be confirmed against it each year.
</Note>

## Quanto Payoff

P&L per contract is the multiplier times the change in the CNY oracle price, paid in USDC:

$$
\text{PnL per contract} = M \times \Delta P_{\text{CNY}}
$$

**Example.** SHAG oracle moves from 1,000 to 1,010 CNY/g. P&L per long contract is
`0.1 × 10 = $1.00`.
