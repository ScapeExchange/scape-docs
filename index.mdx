## Overview

Scape is a HIP-3 deployer on [Hyperliquid](https://hyperliquid.xyz). It operates perpetual
markets on Chinese metals — gold, silver, and copper.

The oracle is CNY-quoted and derived from [Shanghai Metals Market](https://www.metal.com) (SMM)
composite feeds. Margin and settlement are in USDC. There is no CNY/USD conversion at any
point — exposure is quanto.

See [Commodities](/assets/commodities) for the full contract specifications.

## Division of Responsibility

Hyperliquid runs matching, margining, funding, and liquidation. Scape sets the oracle,
market parameters, and market-maker economics.

| Layer | Operator | Function |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Assessment | SMM | CNY composite spot, 500ms cadence |
| Relay | SEDA Protocol | Validated push to Hyperliquid every 3s, no FX conversion |
| Execution | Hyperliquid (HyperCore) | Mark, funding, liquidation, USDC settlement |

## HIP-3

[HIP-3](https://hyperliquid.gitbook.io/hyperliquid-docs) (Hyperliquid Improvement Proposal 3)
allows independent developers to deploy and operate custom perpetual markets on Hyperliquid.
A deployer defines its own market listings, oracle sources, and risk parameters while relying
on Hyperliquid's matching engine, clearinghouse, and validator set for execution and
settlement.

Scape's matching, order types, funding, liquidations, and auto-deleveraging are managed by
HyperCore. Three components are bespoke to Scape — the **oracle price**, the **mark price**,
and the **external price** — computed by the relayer and broadcast to HyperCore. See
[Market Mechanics](/market-mechanics/overview) for details.

## The Scape Interface

[scape.exchange](https://scape.exchange) is an interface for accessing Scape markets. It is
not the exclusive means of access — because Scape markets live on Hyperliquid, they can be
reached through any compatible interface or directly on-chain.
